Alignment

Use flexbox alignment utilities to vertically and horizontally align columns.

Vertical align row data

Classes to align columns. Add these to the row

  • align-items-start
  • align-items-center
  • align-items-end

You may also need to nest the column to get your specific affect.

Align row content to the top

Use .align-items-start in the row to push the content to the top of the row.

  <div class="row align-items-start">
    <div class="col">
      One of three columns
    </div>
    <div class="col">
      One of three columns
    </div>
    <div class="col">
      One of three columns
    </div>
  </div>

Verticall align column data

Add these classes to the 'col', not the row.

  • align-self-start
  • align-self-center
  • align-self-end

These cause the content within the column to be aligned at the top (start), center (center), or bottom (end).

<div class="row">
  <div class="col align-self-start">
        One of three columns
      </div>

      <div class="col align-self-center">
        One of three columns
      </div>

      <div class="col align-self-end">
        One of three columns
      </div>
</div>

Horizontal Alignment

Add these to the row. Make sure to also use a column width.

  • justify-content-start
  • justify-content-center
  • justify-content-end
  • justify-content-around
  • justify-content-between

Align row content to the left

.justify-content-start aligns the row contents to the left.

<div class="row justify-content-start">
  <div class="col-4">
    One of two columns pushed to the left
  </div>

  <div class="col-4">
    One of two columns pushed to the left
  </div>
</div>

Syntax - align-content-(breakpoint)-(align-option)

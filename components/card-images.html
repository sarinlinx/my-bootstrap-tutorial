Image Caps

Image Caps are images at the top or bottom of a card.

Top Image Cap

Place an image at the top of the card using .card-img-top.

<div class="card" style="width: 18rem;">
  <img class="card-img-top" src="..." alt="Card image cap">
  <div class="card-body">
    <p class="card-text">Some quick example text to build on the card title and make up the bulk of the card's content.</p>
  </div>
</div>

Bottom Image Cap

Place an image at the top of the card using .card-img-bottom.

NOTE: You must still place it in the correct position in HTML.

<div class="card" style="width: 18rem;">
<div class="card-body">
<p class="card-text">Some quick example text to build on the card title and make up the bulk of the card's content.</p>
<img class="card-img-bottom" src="..." alt="Card image cap">
</div>
</div>

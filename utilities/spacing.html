Spacing

Spacing refers to margins and padding.

Syntax: (property)-(sides)-(size)

For example: mt-2

Sides

Sides are any of the following:

  • t - top
  • b - bottom
  • l - left
  • r -right
  • x - left and right
  • y - top and bottom
  • blank - all sides

Sizes

Sizes are a percentage of the default rem size.

  • 0 - eliminates size
  • 1 - .25 (rem)
  • 2 - .5 (rem)
  • 3 - 1 (rem)
  • 4 - 1.5 (rem)
  • 5 - 3 (rem)
  • auto - auto (margins only)

Margin/Padding

Syntax - (property m or p)(side t,r,b,l,x,y)-(breakpoint)-(size 0-5 or auto)

A note on the size property.

  • 1 is .25rem
  • 2 is .5rem
  • 3 is 1rem
  • 4 is 1.5rem
  • 5 is 3rem
